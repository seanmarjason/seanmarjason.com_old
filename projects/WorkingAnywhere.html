Gdesi! Where are you working today?

01 January 2021

We have proven over the last 12 months that decentralised, remote working is effective, and yet companies still insist that staff travel into urban hubs to work in 2x2m cubicles that can easily be done elsewhere. So why are so many of us still being forced back into offices, back to our daily commutes, back to the rat race?

gdesi.io - coming soon! [➚]

I'll be honest, I don't have anything close to a solution ... yet. But I'm working on it! I THINK it starts with getting to know more about the issue. More about why we are all forced back into these routines rather than embracing the "new normal".

Thats why gdesi.io now exists. Its not much right now, but just wait and see.

Soon, it'll be THE place you go to embrace the working from anyplace, anytime, anywhere.

Follow gdesi.io as we change the face of "work"!
