About Me

Hi! I’m Sean Marjason – A Business Analyst & Technologist currently based in London, United Kingdom.

Adapting a quote from Transcendence (2014), “So many of us are eager to change the world, but I’ll just settle for understanding it first.”

Our world changes SO quickly, how do we keep up? Let alone make the changes we need to improve how we all live together, and what kind of world we live in. Well, I guess it all starts with information. The more we know about the world, the more we know about the technologies that are becoming prevalent in our society, and the more we talk about their impact on the world, the better equipped we’ll be to take the right steps forward. We need to ensure we focus on the right technologies, and build them in the right way, so that we move forward towards a world we all want to live in.

Right now, I want (and need) to understand the tools and technologies that are changing our society.

That is my focus. That is my passion.
