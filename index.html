About Me

Hi! I’m Sean Marjason – A Business Analyst & Technologist currently based in London, United Kingdom.

Adapting a quote from Transcendence (2014), “So many of us are eager to change the world, but I’ll just settle for understanding it first.”

Change happens so quickly lately, I really don't know how we keep up! And if we can't keep up, how are we meant to get in front of the change and ensure we go in the "right" direction?

For me, it starts with information. The more informed we are, the more equipped we are with knowledge, information, data, the better we can engage and direct change.

My goal is to identify, capture, synthesise, and diseminate information, quickly and correctly. Maybe then we'll be just that little bit more equipped to handle what comes next in this crazy world...

That is my focus. That is my passion.
